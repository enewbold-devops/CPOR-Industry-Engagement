Pillar 3 · AI for Territory Managers

AI that augments judgment — it doesn't replace the gate

Three Copilot Sales Research Agents give Territory Managers a live view of compliance risk and turn it into briefings Account Executives can act on — while every approval stays in human hands.

Alan Newbold | AI Solutions Engineer | E-Newbold Technologies, Inc | developer@e-newbold.com

The three agents

From oversight to action

Each agent answers a different question a Territory Manager faces every week.

Agent 1

Territory Compliance Risk Monitor

"What needs my attention across my territories this week?" Surfaces high-risk, mandatory, and stale obligations before they lapse.

Agent 2

Account Executive Insight Briefing

"What should the AE for Contoso know before the QBR?" Turns catalog risk into account-level talking points and recommended actions.

Agent 3

Approval Queue Situational Awareness

"What's waiting on me, and what's overdue?" A single conversational view of the approval queue with SLA status.

How priorities are set

Risk × time, at a glance

The agents apply one simple model so Territory Managers and AEs always know what to do next.

Escalate to AE now

Mandatory and due within 90 days — immediate action.

Monitor

Mandatory but not yet urgent — quarterly review.

Review

Optional but stale — weekly Territory Manager digest.

Routine

Optional and stable — annual review.

The human gate is preserved. The agents are read-only. Approving or rejecting a registration always happens through the approval flow or the catalog form — never autonomously.

Closing the loop to sellers

Insight that reaches the Account Executive

When an obligation crosses an urgency threshold, the Territory Manager's intelligence flows to the responsible Account Executive as a task in Dynamics 365 Sales — so the right seller acts on the right account at the right time, automatically.
