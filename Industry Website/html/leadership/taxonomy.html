Pillar 1 · Methodology

A governed industry taxonomy your teams can trust

The Industry Catalog turns scattered classification into one authoritative, owned, and auditable system of record — the foundation every regulatory claim is built on.

Alan Newbold | AI Solutions Engineer | E-Newbold Technologies, Inc | developer@e-newbold.com

The model

Three tiers, one source of truth

The catalog is organized so that reference data, mappings, and verified obligations each have a clear home and a clear owner.

Tier 1 · Taxonomy

The vocabulary

33 industry codes mapped to Microsoft Industry Cloud verticals, 20 compliance domains, the territory hierarchy, and authoritative regulatory sources.

Tier 2 · Crosswalk

The mappings

Which compliance domains matter for which industries, and which authoritative sources apply in which territories — maintained as the market evolves.

Tier 3 · Resolution

The verified obligations

Specific regulatory registrations naming the law, regulator, risk rating, and compliance deadline — each verified and approved before it goes live.

Curation lifecycle

Five stages from draft to active

Every registration moves through a governed business process — nothing reaches sellers without verification and a Territory Manager's sign-off.

1

Draft

Analyst captures the legislation and basics.

2

Taxonomy Validation

Industry, territory, and domain confirmed.

3

Legislation Verification

URL, risk, and verified date stamped.

4

Territory Approval

Territory Manager approves or rejects.

5

Activated

Record becomes active and grounds the pipeline.

Trust boundary: only registrations a Territory Manager has approved become active and feed seller-facing intelligence. This is the line between analyst research and what your customers hear.

Ownership

Clear roles, clear accountability

The Global Industry Team operates the catalog through three complementary roles, so curation is never ambiguous.

  • Compliance Analyst — curates registrations and keeps them current
  • Industry Specialist — maintains the industry-to-domain crosswalk
  • Data Steward — runs imports and monitors catalog health

Health & freshness

Stale guidance is worse than none

A 180-day freshness gate and a weekly health digest keep the catalog honest. Records that age out are automatically recycled for re-verification rather than quietly grounding a claim in outdated law.
